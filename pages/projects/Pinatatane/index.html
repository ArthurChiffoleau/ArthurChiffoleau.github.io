Making a multiplayer game

Pinatatane

PITCH

Pinatatane is a 4-player party game, in which you embody a Piñata that fights other Piñatas in order to have the most candies.

You move your Piñata using a third-person view and have two abilities; the first is a dash that let you cover distance quickly as well as pushing other players. The second is a shield that makes you invincible and make other players who dash on you, bounce off away from you.

To win, you have to be the player with most candies at the end of the three minutes round. You gain candies by collecting them when they spawn periodically in the arena or by picking them up when another player dies.

When a player hits a cactus or hit a wall with enough velocity they die, upon death they drop on the ground 25% of their candies. While dead, players cannot pick up candies, they are invincible and can still move but more slowly; after a short period of time they are alive again.

Players must use their dash to project other player into wall or cactus (the cactus stun for a short amount of time) in order to get a chance to pick a part of their candies.
