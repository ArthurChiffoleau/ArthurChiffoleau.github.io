Madlads

This is a catchy title, please don t read it

Pitch

Madlads is a mobile strategy game, where you move and use towers to support your minions to fight against the enemy minions. The goal of the game is to make your minions reach the enemy base so they can destroy it while preventing the enemy from doing the same.

Your minions are organized in squad of four, you can choose the archetypes that will compose upcoming squads and their route. When they have spawned you cannot control them directly. However, what you can do is support them with towers, who buff them, debuff the enemies, make supportive damage to the enemies or transport them to take the advantage. The tower cannot do any damage to enemy base meaning that you will need to make survive your minion until the enemy base. The towers are placed on rail and can be moved along them to follow the minions (or to transport them), you control three or four towers. The towers have passive part that work when the tower is in range of targets and part that need an activation from player that unleash diverse ability.

The challenges are to compose a good squad of minion that will counter the enemy. Then, you need to choose a good route that will allow your minions to encounter enemies at good position. Finally, you need to support them with good tower positioning and use of abilities.
