Creating a boardgame

Mission Hermes

PITCH

We are in 2050, the race to spatial colonization is in full swing. You are at the head of a company who has sensed the potential profits that could be made on Mars, but you are not alone to be attracted by the gains. Only one company will be victorious of this Mars rush. And you cannot conceive to let the others win and that's good because just like you the others are without faith or laws. To take the victory there is no holds barred, may it be false alliances, betrayals or dirty deals; this will be your daily life.

Your objective is to generate as much profit (in Credits) as you can, by exploiting Mars resources. The credits gained will allow you to buy economics centers and shares on Earth. To make it to your end, you will take into account the fluctuations of the stock market, the different natural events and the other players who might try to buy your company actions.

ROLE

On this project I was Game Designer in a team of five peoples. My missions were the following:

  • Creating the rules of the game,
  • Making paper prototypes,
  • Observating and analyzing our playtests,
  • Writing the rulebook of the game.
