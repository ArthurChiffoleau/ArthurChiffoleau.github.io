GAME JAMS AND OTHER PROJECTS

KILLEARTH

Project realized during Ludum Dare 42 in a team of 4 persons, puzzle reflection game where you have to manage overpopulation on a planet.

ALIEN EXHIBITION

Project realized during Global 2018 within a team of 6 persons, platformer in cooperation where you have to resolve small puzzles to make progress your teammate.

La course du 3 ème âge

Project realized during a Game Jam within a team of 6 persons, a local-multiplayer racing game where you play an inhabitant of a retirement home that race against other residents. You play in a wheelchair, where you control each wheel with a stick of the joystick and you have to be the first to crossed the finish line.

REMAKE DE METROID

A class exercise were we remake an old game and add a mechanic. In our group we decided to remake the beginning of the first metroid add the ability to inverse the gravity.

Space-corners

A game realized during the Game Maker Toolkit (GMTK) Game jam within a team of 4 people, a shoot up where you automatically shoot and always go in the direction of one rectangle corner's and you can switch to go in direction of the next corner.
