GAME JAM AND OTHER PROJECT

KILLEARTH

Project realised during Ludum Dare 42 in a team of 4 person, puzzle reflexion game where you have to manage overpopulation on a planet.

ALIEN EXHIBITION

Project realised during Global 2018 in a team of 6 persons, platformer in cooperation where you have to resolve small puzzle to make progress your teamate.

La course du 3 ème âge

Project realised during a Game Jam within a team of 6 persons, a local-multiplayer racing game where you play an inhabitant of a retirement home that race against other resident. You play in a wheelchair, where you control each wheel with a stick of the joystick and you have to be the first to crossed the finish line.

REMAKE DE METROID

We make excercise in class where we have to remake an old game and add a mechanic. In our group we decided to remake the begining of the first metroid add the ability to inverse the gravity.

space-corners

A game realised during the Game Maker Toolkit (GMTK) Game jam within a team of 4 people, a shoot up where you automaticly shoot and always go in the direction of one rectangle corner's and you can switch to go in direction of the next corner.
