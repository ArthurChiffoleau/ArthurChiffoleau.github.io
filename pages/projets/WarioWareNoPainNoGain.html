PROJECT

We have been assigned to make a Wario -Ware game as a class project. We were 40, each of us was at charge to make two documentations for two mini-games, then we had to develop two mini-games conceived by someone else by following their documentations. In addition of their mini-games, some people have taken the responsability of the realisation of the game's structure which englobs the mini-games. I was part of this team as a Game Designer and a Sound Designer.

Mini-Games designed

I have designed two mini-games: Angry Wario Kid and Save America. In Angry Wario Kid, you have to destroy a virtual keyboard by smashing the keys of your actual keyboard. In Save America, you have to cut the good wires on a bomb to diffuse it and save your great country America.

MINI-GAMES MAde

I have programmed two mini-games: Cave-Digger and It leans by following the documentation as accurately as possible. Cave-digger is a game where you have to make your path to the treasure by digging the blocks. You do so by pressing the corresponding keys on your keyboard but be careful about the lava! In It Leans you have to move your character in order to balance the incoming weights in order not to fall.
